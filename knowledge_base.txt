Customer Support Knowledge Base

Refund Policy:
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
To initiate a refund, customers must provide their order ID and reason for return.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Shipping Policy:
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs an additional $15.
Free shipping is available on orders above $50.

Account Issues:
To reset your password, click on forgot password on the login page.
If your account is locked, contact support at support@company.com.
Account verification emails are sent within 5 minutes of registration.

Product Issues:
If you receive a damaged product, take a photo and contact support within 48 hours.
Warranty claims can be filed within 1 year of purchase.
Technical support is available Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm.
